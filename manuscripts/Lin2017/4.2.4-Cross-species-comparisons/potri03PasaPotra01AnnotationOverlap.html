2.3 Missing Potri genes

We simply look for different cutoff of coverage and identity (70,80 and 90%), how many genes align that do not overlap with existing annotation with a minimum overlap of 30 or 70%

res <- mapply(function(threshold,ovl){
  ci.sel <- df$coverage >= threshold & df$identity >= threshold
  sel <- !df[ci.sel,"Name"] %in% IDs[[ovl]]
  write(as.character(df[ci.sel,"Name"][sel]),file=file.path("../analysis/BEDTools",
  paste("Potri03-gene-aligned-Potra01-coverage-identity",threshold,
        "percent-no",gsub("ID|p","",ovl),"percent-Potra01-PASA-overlap.txt",sep="-")))
  return(sum(sel))
},rep(c(70,80,90),2),rep(c("ID30p","ID70p"),each=3))

res <- matrix(res,nrow=2,byrow=TRUE)
dimnames(res) <- list(c("ID30p","ID70p"),c(70,80,90))

pander(res)
70 80 90
ID30p 8112 7483 5285
ID70p 13048 12270 9307

3 Conclusion

At a 90% coverage and identity cutoff, 5,285 Potri gene align to the Potra genome but do not overlap an existing annotation by at least 30 percent.
